Admissions Process

Begin your journey at Springfield Academy.

How to Apply

We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and contribute to our vibrant school community. Our admissions process is designed to be thorough and thoughtful, ensuring a good fit for both the student and the school.

Application Steps

  1. Submit Online Application: Complete and submit our online application form along with the non-refundable application fee.
  2. Document Submission: Provide copies of previous school transcripts, report cards, and any standardized test scores.
  3. Student Assessment: Prospective students will be invited to participate in an age-appropriate assessment to gauge academic readiness.
  4. Family Interview: We conduct a friendly interview with the student and parents/guardians to get to know you better.

Important Dates

  • Applications Open: October 1st
  • Application Deadline: January 31st
  • Decision Notification: March 15th
